Lipella Pharmaceuticals Inc.

Lipella Pharmaceuticals Inc. was a clinical-stage biotechnology company headquartered in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, focused on developing novel therapies by reformulating established active pharmaceutical ingredients using proprietary liposomal drug delivery technology. Founded in 2005, the company advanced multiple product candidates through preclinical and clinical development targeting diseases with significant unmet medical need.

The company's lead clinical programs included therapies for oral lichen planus, hemorrhagic cystitis, oral graft-versus-host disease, and non-muscle invasive bladder cancer. Lipella also developed intellectual property relating to the diagnosis of interstitial cystitis and completed multiple FDA-authorized clinical studies.

In 2022, Lipella completed its initial public offering, and its common stock traded on the Nasdaq Capital Market under the ticker symbol LIPO.

On March 30, 2026, Lipella filed a voluntary petition for relief under Chapter 11 of the United States Bankruptcy Code in the United States Bankruptcy Court for the Western District of Pennsylvania (Case No. 26-20907). Following a court-supervised sale process, the company completed the sale of substantially all of its operating assets.
