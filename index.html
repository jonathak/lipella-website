Lipella Pharmaceuticals Inc.

Notice: Lipella Pharmaceuticals Inc. completed the sale of substantially all of its operating assets in June 2026 and is proceeding through a Chapter 11 liquidation. This website is maintained for historical reference and ongoing public disclosures.

Lipella Pharmaceuticals Inc. operated as a clinical-stage biotechnology company headquartered in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. The Company developed novel therapies using proprietary liposomal drug delivery technology and advanced multiple drug candidates into FDA-authorized clinical trials, including Phase 2 studies in oral lichen planus and hemorrhagic cystitis. The Company obtained multiple Investigational New Drug authorizations from the U.S. Food and Drug Administration.

The Company's lead indications included oral lichen planus, hemorrhagic cystitis, oral graft-versus-host disease, and non-muscle invasive bladder cancer. Lipella also developed intellectual property relating to the diagnosis of interstitial cystitis and completed multiple FDA-authorized clinical studies.

In 2022, Lipella completed its initial public offering, and its common stock began trading on the Nasdaq Capital Market under the ticker symbol LIPO.

On March 30, 2026, Lipella filed a voluntary petition for relief under Chapter 11 of the United States Bankruptcy Code in the United States Bankruptcy Court for the Western District of Pennsylvania (Case No. 26-20907). Following a court-approved sale process under Section 363 of the Bankruptcy Code, the Company completed the sale of substantially all of its operating assets to XRAIY LLC, which acquired substantially all of the Company's operating assets with the intention of continuing development of the Company's therapeutic programs. Following completion of the asset sale, Lipella no longer conducts research and development activities and is winding down its remaining affairs through the Chapter 11 process.

This website is maintained as the historical archive of Lipella Pharmaceuticals Inc. During the Company's wind-down and liquidation process, it will continue to serve as the Company's public website for notices, announcements, and other public disclosures. Historical publications, press releases, SEC filings, and other corporate information will remain available.
