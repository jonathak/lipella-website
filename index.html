Lipella Pharmaceuticals Inc.

Founded in 2005, Lipella Pharmaceuticals Inc. was a clinical-stage biotechnology company headquartered in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. The Company developed novel therapies using proprietary liposomal drug delivery technology and advanced multiple product candidates from laboratory research into FDA-authorized clinical trials, including Phase 2 studies in oral lichen planus and hemorrhagic cystitis. The Company obtained multiple Investigational New Drug authorizations from the U.S. Food and Drug Administration.

The Company's lead indications included oral lichen planus, hemorrhagic cystitis, oral graft-versus-host disease, and non-muscle invasive bladder cancer. Lipella also developed intellectual property relating to the diagnosis of interstitial cystitis and completed multiple FDA-authorized clinical studies.

In 2022, Lipella completed its initial public offering, and its common stock traded on the Nasdaq Capital Market under the ticker symbol LIPO.

On March 30, 2026, Lipella filed a voluntary petition for relief under Chapter 11 of the United States Bankruptcy Code in the United States Bankruptcy Court for the Western District of Pennsylvania (Case No. 26-20907). Following a court-approved sale process under Section 363 of the Bankruptcy Code, the Company completed the sale of substantially all of its operating assets to XRAIY LLC, which acquired the assets for continued development.
